1. Virgin of the Birds - Fatal Flower Garden

“GAUDY WOMAN LURES CHILD FROM PLAYFELLOWS; STABS HIM AS VICTIM DICTATES MESSAGE TO PARENTS”
Recorded and performed by Virgin of the Birds. Virgin of the Birds is Jon Rooney.
With “Fatal Flower Garden” I tried to make it sound as far from folk or Americana as possible, thus the electric guitars, distorted synthesizers and general fuzziness.
